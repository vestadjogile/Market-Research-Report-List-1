<p><p>The Aircraft Defibrillators market is categorized into three primary types: Automatic Type, Manual Type, and Semi-Automatic Type. Automatic defibrillators analyze a patient's heart rhythm and deliver shocks without user intervention, ensuring immediate assistance during emergencies. Manual types require trained personnel to interpret heart rhythms and administer shocks, offering more control in critical situations. Semi-Automatic types guide users through the defibrillation process, combining accessibility with basic user input. Together, these types address varying needs for in-flight emergency medical responses.</p></p>
