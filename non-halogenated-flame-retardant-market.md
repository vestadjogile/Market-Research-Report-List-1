<p><p>The non-halogenated flame retardant market comprises several key types: phosphorous, nitrogenous, and other materials. Phosphorous-based flame retardants enhance fire resistance by promoting char formation and reducing flammability, making them suitable for various applications like textiles and plastics. Nitrogenous flame retardants, which work through gas-phase mechanisms, release nitrogen oxides during combustion to hinder flame propagation. Other market segments include inorganic compounds and intumescent systems, which expand when exposed to heat, creating a protective barrier against flames.</p></p>
