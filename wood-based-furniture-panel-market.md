<p><p>The wood-based furniture panel market includes several key types: </p><p>- **Particleboard (PB)** is made from wood chips and adhesive, offering an economical option for furniture. </p><p>- **Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF)**, composed of wood fibers, is versatile and ideal for detailed designs.  </p><p>- **High Density Fibreboard (HDF)** is denser than MDF, providing durability for flooring and furniture.  </p><p>- **Oriented Strand Board (OSB)** consists of layered wood strands, known for strength and structural applications.  </p><p>- **Plywood**, made of layers of veneer, is renowned for its stability and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
