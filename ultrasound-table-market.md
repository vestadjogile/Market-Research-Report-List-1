<p><p>The ultrasound table market features three primary types: electric, hydraulic, and manual ultrasound tables. Electric ultrasound tables offer adjustable heights and positions at the touch of a button, enhancing convenience and efficiency. Hydraulic ultrasound tables utilize a pump mechanism for adjustments, providing stability and ease of use, ideal for precise imaging procedures. Manual ultrasound tables rely on manual adjustments for positioning and height, catering to cost-sensitive settings while still providing fundamental functionality for ultrasound examinations.</p></p>
