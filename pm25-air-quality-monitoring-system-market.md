<p><p>The PM2.5 air quality monitoring system market is categorized into several types: TEOM (Tapered Element Oscillating Microbalance) monitors, which provide real-time measurements of particulate matter by weighing collected particles; Beta Attenuation Monitors, which assess PM2.5 levels based on the attenuation of beta radiation as it passes through airborne particles; and other types, which include a variety of methods such as laser optical and gravimetric techniques. These systems are essential for evaluating air quality and public health impacts.</p></p>
