<p><strong>The Semiconductor Single Crystal Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CZ Single Crystal Furnace</li><li>FZ Single Crystal Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor Single Crystal Furnace market comprises mainly two types: CZ (Czochralski) and FZ (Floating Zone) Single Crystal Furnaces. CZ furnaces are widely used for growing silicon crystals by melting silicon and then pulling a single crystal seed from the melt, making them ideal for mass production. In contrast, FZ furnaces utilize a zone melting technique that allows for higher purity and better control over crystal properties, catering to specialized applications where quality is paramount.</p></p>
