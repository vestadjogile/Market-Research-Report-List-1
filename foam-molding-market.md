<p><strong>The Foam Molding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed-cell Foam</li><li>Open-cell Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foam molding market can be divided into two primary types: closed-cell foam and open-cell foam. Closed-cell foam features tightly packed cells that do not allow air or moisture to penetrate, making it ideal for insulation, flotation devices, and applications requiring resistance to water. In contrast, open-cell foam consists of interconnected cells, providing breathability and softness, making it suitable for cushioning, soundproofing, and bedding products. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and healthcare.</p></p>
