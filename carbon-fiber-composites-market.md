<p><p>The carbon fiber composites market encompasses various types, primarily categorized by their matrix materials. Carbon fiber-metal composites combine metals like aluminum or titanium with carbon fibers, offering improved mechanical performance for structural components. Carbon fiber-ceramic composites exhibit high thermal resistance and are ideal for applications in high-temperature environments. Other composites may include innovative blends tailored for specific performance needs across diverse industries.</p></p>
