<p><p>The rockery stone market is divided into two primary types: natural stone and artificial stone. Natural stone consists of materials like granite, limestone, and sandstone, prized for their durability and aesthetic appeal. These stones are sourced directly from quarries and have unique textures and colors. In contrast, artificial stone is engineered from a combination of resins, aggregates, and pigments, allowing for customizable shapes and patterns. Both types cater to landscaping and garden design, enhancing outdoor aesthetics while serving functional purposes.</p></p>
