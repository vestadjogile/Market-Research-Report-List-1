<p><p>The Base Station RF Duplexer market can be categorized into three main types: Crystal Type, Ceramics Type, and Others. Crystal Type duplexers utilize crystal filters for high-frequency performance and stability, making them ideal for applications requiring precise signal separation. Ceramics Type duplexers employ ceramic materials, offering robustness and better thermal stability, suitable for a wide range of operating conditions. The "Others" category includes hybrid and custom duplexers, which may combine elements from both types or incorporate unique designs to meet specific requirements.</p></p>
