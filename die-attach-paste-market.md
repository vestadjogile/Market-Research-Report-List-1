<p><p>Die attach paste is essential in semiconductor packaging, offering various types for specific applications. No-clean pastes reduce post-assembly cleaning, ideal for high-speed production. Rosin-based pastes provide excellent adhesion and thermal stability but require cleaning. Water-soluble pastes are easy to clean with water but may need careful handling. Other types may include specialty formulations for unique bonding requirements. Each type balances adhesion, thermal conductivity, and process efficiency to meet the diverse needs of the electronics industry.</p></p>
