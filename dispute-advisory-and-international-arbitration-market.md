<p><p>Dispute advisory and international arbitration refer to specialized services that help resolve disputes between parties, especially in cross-border transactions. Dispute advisory encompasses pre-litigation discussions, mediation, and negotiation strategies, aimed at preventing disputes or steering them toward amicable resolutions. Companies are increasingly opting for arbitration to avoid the uncertainties of national courts and to benefit from expertise in handling complex disputes. Additionally, many jurisdictions are enhancing their legal frameworks to support arbitration, further stimulating demand. The trend toward remote arbitration proceedings, accelerated by the COVID-19 pandemic, is expected to continue, providing greater flexibility and efficiency. The Dispute Advisory and International Arbitration Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period, reflecting the rising need for effective dispute resolution mechanisms in a complex global business environment.</p></p>
