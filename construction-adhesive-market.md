<p><p>The construction adhesive market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Acrylic adhesives offer quick setting and versatility, ideal for various surfaces. Polyurethane adhesives provide strong bonding and flexibility, making them suitable for outdoor use. Polyvinyl acetate (PVA) is often used in woodwork due to its strong adhesion and ease of cleanup. Epoxy adhesives are known for their exceptional strength and resistance to environmental factors, making them ideal for structural applications. Other adhesives may include silicone and hybrid formulations, catering to niche needs.</p></p>
