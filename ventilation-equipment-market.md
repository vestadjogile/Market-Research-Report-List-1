<p><strong>The Ventilation Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Filter</li><li>Air Handling Unit (AHU)</li><li>Air Purifier</li><li>Roof Vent</li><li>Axial Fan</li><li>Centrifugal Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ventilation equipment market includes various types essential for maintaining indoor air quality and climate control. Air filters remove pollutants from the air, while air handling units (AHUs) regulate and circulate air within buildings. Air purifiers focus on eliminating airborne contaminants. Roof vents facilitate natural airflow, enhancing ventilation for spaces below. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan shaft, ideal for smaller spaces, while centrifugal fans generate high pressure for larger systems, efficiently distributing air in diverse environments.</p></p>
