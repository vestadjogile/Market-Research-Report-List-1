<p><p>The automotive load sensor market includes several types, each serving specific measurement needs. Wheel spoke sensors measure the load on vehicle wheels, enhancing safety and performance. Torque transducers monitor torque levels in various components, ensuring efficient power transmission. Hole sensors detect forces acting on structural components, contributing to vehicle stability. S-sensors offer versatile applications by measuring not only load but also acceleration and displacement. Together, these sensors play a vital role in optimizing vehicle dynamics and safety systems.</p></p>
