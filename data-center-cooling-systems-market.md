<p><strong>The Data Center Cooling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Cooled Precision Air Conditioning</li><li>Centrifugal Water Cooled Air Conditioning System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Data center cooling systems are essential for maintaining optimal operating temperatures in IT environments. Air-cooled precision air conditioning uses refrigerants to cool air, typically installed as standalone units, ensuring precise temperature control. Conversely, centrifugal water-cooled systems utilize chilled water produced by large chillers, circulating it to cool air within the data center. This method is more efficient for larger facilities, offering lower operating costs but requiring extensive plumbing and infrastructure, making it suitable for larger-scale data center operations.</p></p>
