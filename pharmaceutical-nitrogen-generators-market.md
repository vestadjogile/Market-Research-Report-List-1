<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Nitrogen Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Swing Adsorption</li><li>Membrane</li><li>Cryogenic Air</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical nitrogen generators market comprises three main types: Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA), Membrane, and Cryogenic Air. PSA technology utilizes adsorption processes to separate nitrogen from air under varying pressure, making it suitable for high purity applications. Membrane technology employs selective permeation through polymer membranes, offering a more compact and energy-efficient solution. Cryogenic air separation involves cooling air to liquefy it, then distilling it to isolate nitrogen, providing large-scale production of high-purity nitrogen for pharmaceutical uses.</p></p>
