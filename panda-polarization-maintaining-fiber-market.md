<p><p>Panda polarization maintaining (PM) fibers are categorized into single-mode and multimode types, each serving distinct applications. Single-mode PM fibers support one light path, ensuring minimal signal degradation over long distances, suitable for telecommunications and sensors. In contrast, multimode PM fibers allow multiple light paths, providing higher data capacity over shorter distances, often used in data centers and local networks. The choice between these types depends on factors such as distance, bandwidth requirements, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
