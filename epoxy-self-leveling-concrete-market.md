<p><p>The epoxy self-leveling concrete market is divided into two main types: concrete underlayment and epoxy self-leveling concrete coating. Concrete underlayment serves as a preparatory layer, providing a smooth and even surface for subsequent flooring installations. In contrast, epoxy self-leveling concrete coating is applied directly to floors, offering a durable, glossy finish that enhances aesthetics and protects against wear and chemical damage. Both variations cater to different flooring needs in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
