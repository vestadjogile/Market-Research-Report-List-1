<p><ul><li>Alkaline (ALK) Electrolyzer</li><li>Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Electrolyzer</li><li>Solid Oxide Electrolyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The green hydrogen market consists of three main electrolyzer types: Alkaline (ALK), Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM), and Solid Oxide Electrolyzer. ALK electrolyzers are cost-effective and suitable for large-scale production, relying on liquid alkaline solutions. PEM electrolyzers offer high efficiency and rapid response times, making them ideal for variable renewable energy sources. Solid Oxide Electrolyzers operate at high temperatures, allowing for improved efficiency and utilization of waste heat. Each technology serves distinct applications and energy requirements in the growing green hydrogen sector.</p></p>
