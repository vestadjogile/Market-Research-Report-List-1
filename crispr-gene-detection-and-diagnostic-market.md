<p><strong>The CRISPR Gene Detection and Diagnostic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cutting Function Detection Method</li><li>Positioning Function Detection Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CRISPR gene detection and diagnostic market can be categorized into two main types: Cutting Function Detection Method and Positioning Function Detection Method. The Cutting Function Detection Method utilizes CRISPR's ability to cleave specific DNA sequences, enabling precise identification of genetic material associated with diseases. In contrast, the Positioning Function Detection Method focuses on the targeting ability of CRISPR to bind specific sequences without cutting, allowing for the detection of genetic variations and pathogens, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and versatility.</p></p>
