<p><ul><li>Lump Steam Coal</li><li>Steam Coal Fines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam coal market comprises various types, primarily lump steam coal and steam coal fines. Lump steam coal consists of larger, homogeneous chunks, ideally suited for power generation and industrial applications due to its higher calorific value and lower impurities. Conversely, steam coal fines are smaller particles that arise during the mining and handling processes. While fines have lower energy content and can be more challenging to utilize, they are often blended with lump coal to optimize combustion efficiency and reduce costs.</p></p>
