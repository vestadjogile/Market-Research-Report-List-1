<p><p>The Battery Burst Vent market features two primary types: Thimble Blast Type and Piston Spring Type. Thimble Blast Type vents utilize a cylindrical design that allows for the rapid release of gases in an emergency, enhancing safety by preventing battery rupture. In contrast, Piston Spring Type vents employ a spring mechanism that opens to release pressure when it exceeds safe levels, providing controlled venting. Both types are crucial for maintaining safety and prolonging the lifecycle of batteries in various applications.</p></p>
