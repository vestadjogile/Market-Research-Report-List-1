<p><ul><li>Single Pocket</li><li>Rotary Pocket</li><li>Linear Pocket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E-beam evaporation systems are key in thin-film deposition, with various market types catering to different production needs. Single pocket systems are compact and suitable for low-volume applications, ensuring precise control over material deposition. Rotary pocket systems enhance efficiency by allowing multiple materials to be deposited in a continuous process, ideal for medium to high-volume production. Linear pocket systems offer a larger deposition area, facilitating high-throughput applications, and are suitable for large substrates, making them versatile for various industries.</p></p>
