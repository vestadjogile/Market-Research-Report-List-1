<p><p>The electromagnetic wave shield film market comprises two main types: metal-mesh films and coating films. Metal-mesh films feature a network of conductive metallic strands, effectively blocking electromagnetic interference while maintaining transparency. Coating films, on the other hand, involve applying a special conductive layer to a substrate, providing similar shielding properties but often at a lower cost. Both types are utilized across various industries, such as electronics and automotive, to enhance device performance and protect sensitive components from EM radiation.</p></p>
