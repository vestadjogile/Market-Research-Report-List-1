<p><p>The smartphone speaker market is categorized into three primary types: high sensitivity, low sensitivity, and others. High sensitivity speakers are designed to produce louder sound with minimal power, enhancing audio quality in compact devices. Low sensitivity speakers, on the other hand, require more power to achieve comparable volume, which may impact battery efficiency. The "others" category encompasses specialized or hybrid designs that combine features from both high and low sensitivity speakers, catering to niche applications and user preferences in mobile audio technology.</p></p>
