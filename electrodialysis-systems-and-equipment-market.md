<p><strong>The Electrodialysis Systems and Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Electrodialysis</li><li>Batch Electrodialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrodialysis systems utilize ionic membranes to separate charged particles in solutions, driven by an electric field. Continuous electrodialysis operates continuously, allowing for the ongoing removal of ions from feed solutions, making it efficient for large-scale applications like desalination and wastewater treatment. In contrast, batch electrodialysis processes are conducted in discrete batches, offering flexibility and ease of operation for smaller volumes or specialized applications. Both types serve critical roles in water purification and resource recovery in various industries.</p></p>
