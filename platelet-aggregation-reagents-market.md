<p><p>The platelet aggregation reagents market includes various agents that facilitate the study of platelet function and interactions. ADP (adenosine diphosphate) is a key agonist promoting platelet activation and aggregation. Acetylsalicylic Acid, commonly known as aspirin, inhibits platelet aggregation by affecting cyclooxygenase enzymes. Prostaglandins, lipid compounds, influence platelet aggregation and vascular functions. Other reagents encompass a range of substances used in specialized assays to assess platelet function, contributing to research and clinical diagnostics in cardiovascular health and blood disorders.</p></p>
