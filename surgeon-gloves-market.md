<p><p>The surgeon gloves market comprises various materials, each serving distinct purposes. Latex gloves offer excellent flexibility and comfort, making them a popular choice in surgical environments, although they may cause allergies in some users. Vinyl gloves are cost-effective and suitable for low-risk procedures, although they provide less sensitivity. Nitrile rubber gloves are highly durable and resistant to chemicals and punctures, making them ideal for high-risk tasks. Neoprene gloves blend chemical resistance with flexibility, catering to specialized medical needs. Each type addresses specific procedural demands and user preferences.</p></p>
